# Mapping shapefile resources for the Pacific Islands Region

Links to potentially useful shapefiles for glider mission planning and map generation. 

### Stock boundaries for managed cetacean species

Includes bottlenose dolphins, false killer whales, melon headed whales, spinner dolphins, spotted dolphins  

[https://github.com/PIFSC-Protected-Species-Division/HI-cet-stock-boundaries](https://github.com/PIFSC-Protected-Species-Division/HI-cet-stock-boundaries)



### Pelagic false killer whale management area

Oleson et al. 2023 Developing a management area for Hawai‘i pelagic false killer whales  

[https://github.com/PIFSC-Protected-Species-Division/HI-Pelagic-FKWs](https://github.com/PIFSC-Protected-Species-Division/HI-Pelagic-FKWs)


### Longline prohibited areas

[https://geoportal.hawaii.gov/datasets/HiStateGIS::longline-prohibited-areas/about](https://geoportal.hawaii.gov/datasets/HiStateGIS::longline-prohibited-areas/about)


## pic-maps

R package that has many relevant polygons pre-packaged and easy to call with built in functions. Polygons included Hawai‘i EEZ, CNMI EEZ, Papahānaumokuākea Marine National Monument, American Samoa US EEZ, Howland and Baker Islands US EEZ, Jarvis Islands US EEZ, Johnston Atoll US EEZ, Palmyra and Kingman US EEZ, Wake Island US EEZ, Pelagic FKW Management Area, Longline exlusion zones, Northwestern Hawaiian Islands Coastline
